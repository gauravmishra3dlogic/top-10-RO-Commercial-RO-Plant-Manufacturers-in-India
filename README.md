## 📌 What is a Commercial RO Plant?

A commercial RO (Reverse Osmosis) plant is a water purification system designed to remove dissolved salts, impurities, and contaminants from water at a larger scale.

These systems are commonly used in:
- Hotels & Restaurants  
- Hospitals & Clinics  
- Educational Institutions  
- Manufacturing Industries  
- Corporate Offices

## 🔍 Key Factors to Choose the Best RO Manufacturer

Before selecting a commercial RO plant manufacturer, the following factors should be considered:

### ✔ Water Quality (TDS Level)
The system should be selected based on raw water quality and contamination level.

### ✔ Plant Capacity
Choose capacity based on daily water consumption (LPH requirement).

### ✔ After-Sales Service
Strong service support is essential for long-term performance.

### ✔ Customization Options
Industries require tailored solutions for better efficiency.

### ✔ Cost & Maintenance
Both initial cost and AMC charges should be evaluated.
